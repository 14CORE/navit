333  *
334  * 31 30 23 22 0
335  * +--------+---------------+---------------+
336  * | s 1bit | e[30:23] 8bit | f[22:0] 23bit |
337  * +--------+---------------+---------------+
338  * B0------------------->B1------->B2-->B3-->
339  *
340  * IEEE Standard 754 Double Precision Storage Format (gdouble):
341  *
342  * 63 62 52 51 32 31 0
